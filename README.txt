Control your ABB and Busch-Jaeger free@home lights directly from Homey. The app connects to your free@home System Access Point over your local network instead of through the cloud, so your lights keep responding instantly, even when your internet connection is down.

Once set up, your free@home lights join the rest of your smart home in Homey. Turn them on or off, dim them, and bring them into your Flows so they work together with all your other devices and automations from one place.
