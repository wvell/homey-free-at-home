free@home Local

A Homey Pro app for the ABB / Busch-Jaeger free@home System Access Point. It
connects directly over your local network — no cloud — so your home keeps
responding even when the internet is down.

Bring your free@home lighting into Homey and use it together with the rest of
your smart home and your automations, all from one place.

Unofficial app, not affiliated with or endorsed by ABB or Busch-Jaeger. Device
mapping is based on the MIT-licensed homebridge-freeathome-local-api by Philip
Gerke (see LICENSE).
